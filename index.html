Link's Rupee Game

INSTRUCTIONS:

Click on the button to generate a random number. You have four rupees to choose from that will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, and you lose the game if your score goes above the generated number.

The value of each rupee is hidden until you click on it. Each time you lose your total score will restart and you have to generate another random number.
